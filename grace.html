GRACE / year 2 / Sand storm

drawings

To live is to experience entropy.

Every action has its own reaction and it isn't always going to be a bullseye. You can control the direction you want to go, but things won't fall into your lap without a little wrestle.

We'll try and try again but like the sand in the box, there will always be forces that move of their own accord. You have no choice but to go with the flow.
