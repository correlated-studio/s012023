about

Students will be required to design, document, fabricate, and plan the exhibition of their individual installations utilising architectural communication techniques.

Throughout the semester, students can expect to engage with -and refer to- topics and disciplines outside of the architecture realm including: fundamental physics theories, art, and web design, amongst others. This semester's driving topic is quantum entanglement.
