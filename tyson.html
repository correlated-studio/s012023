TYSON LEE / year 3 / The Spatial Conversation

Explore the intersection of time and architecture in a controlled environment.

Within a minimalist box, users manipulate mirrors to influence the display of a split video projected onto the back screen. The video, divided into upper and lower halves, responds to the mirrors' adjustments, offering an insightful study on the correlation between spatial configurations and temporal perceptions.

This project provides a hands-on experience, allowing users to engage with the dynamic relationship between space and time through controlled manipulation of reflective elements.
