WANFENG / year 2/ Time Dilation

This art installation is a creative idea inspired by Einstein's theory of relativity. It consists of a box and a rotating handle, with a built-in projection device inside the box. Through interaction with the handle, the audience can personally experience the relativity of time.

When the handle is rotated downwards, the projected images slow down, displaying the effect of time dilation, creating a sense of entering a slowly passing spacetime. Conversely, when the handle is rotated upwards, the projected images accelerate, showing the speeding up of time, prompting the audience to explore and contemplate time and space.

This art installation not only showcases the beauty of scientific theories but also stimulates profound reflections on time, space, and human perception. Through touch, rotation, and observation, viewers can intuitively feel the relativity of time, sparking their curiosity about the mysteries of the universe.
