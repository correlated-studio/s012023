HARISH JAIGANESH RAVEE/ year 32/ PARALLEL AWARENESS

"The Clock highlights the centrality of time within conventional cinema narratives – the way it binds stories and leads us through their events.

Both an art work and a time piece, the video heightens time and tells us the time. It creates an uncanny correspondence between cinematic and real time, drawing viewers into a parallel awareness of what they watch on screen and experience beyond it."
