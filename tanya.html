tanya / year 2 / Converging Divergence

Convergence- When objects or ideas come together to form a whole.

The macroscopic experience of time is sequential and linear. To engage with the object users must take a series of actions to view the projection with clarity. The steps to view the projection can only be actioned in a sequence, and one step cannot be completed before the other; drawing parallels with how moments in time are experienced chronologically.

Once the object comes together as a whole its convergence is complete, and divergence can be experienced. The universes incomparable expansion and acceleration through time is one beyond the limits of the human experience; through the video, users can reflect on the juxtaposition between the human life cycle and its almost negligible occupation of time and space in the entirety of the universe.
