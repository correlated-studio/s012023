QAMAR AL-HASANI / year 2 / Home in Three

drawings

What is home to you?

Three cities. three timelines, three events unfold before you, dragging time to encapsulate it. Home and nostalgia are not bound by linear chronology, but rather, converge, overlap, and coexist in one object. Home is a name, a word that is personal and experiential. Time is in the eye of the observer, there it becomes a malleable entity, bent and moulded by personal perception.

The project mirrors humanity's innate curiosity to peer into the folds of time, to interact with the echoes of history and to ultimately reminisce home.
